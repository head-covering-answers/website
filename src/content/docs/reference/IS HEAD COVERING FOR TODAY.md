### QUESTION: Do Paul's instructions about head covering still apply to Christian women nowadays?

### REASON FOR THIS QUESTION:

- Most people haven't thought through the New Testament teaching about head covering (nor received any teaching on the topic).

- Most Christian women don't wear head coverings in today's world. Based on that, it could easily appear that head covering is not necessary.

### SHORT ANSWER:  Yes.

### REASON FOR THE ANSWER:

### Part 1

- Paul says that it's for all churches (1 Corinthians 11:16)
