- Most Christian women don't wear head coverings in today's world.
- So it would  easily appear that head covering is not necessary.
- Also missing: pastors preaching on this passage, people reading & studying this passage.


### SHORT ANSWER: Yes.

### REASON FOR THE ANSWER:

- Paul says that it's for all churches (1 Corinthians 11:16)
- People's behavior may change over time, but the Bible hasn't changed.
